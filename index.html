About Me

Data Architect | Charity Trustee | BCS Board Member


As a Data Architect, I specialise in designing and maintaining robust data infrastructures while setting critical policies for data protection, retention, and ethics. My journey began as a Data Analyst, where I honed my skills in data presentation, developing a passion for delivering clear and impactful insights. This passion continues to drive my approach to data strategy and communication today.


To complement and expand my data expertise, I am currently studying Data Science at BPP University, further enriching my skills and rounding out my data skillset.


Beyond my professional role, I am deeply committed to using my expertise to make a meaningful difference. As a trustee for two LGBTQIA+ Charities, I leverage my knowledge in data, technology, finance, and strategic risk management to support their growth and amplify their impact within the community. Serving as Treasurer and Risk Committee Chair in these organisations allows me to apply sound financial oversight and risk mitigation strategies to advance their missions.


My commitment to positive change in the tech industry extends through my active involvement with the British Computer Society. As a member of the BCS Community Board and Policy Engagement Officer on the Early Careers Executive Committee, I am devoted to promoting diversity in technology and ensuring that IT serves the greater good across the UK.


Driven by a passion for impactful data storytelling and a commitment to inclusive leadership, I strive to bridge technology and community to foster meaningful progress.
